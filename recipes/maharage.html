maharage

Description

maharage is is beans based food, used as a soup supportin various dishes

Ingredients

  • 0.5kg of beans
  • one carrot, one bell pepper
  • 0.1 litre of coconut milk
  • 5 one piece of garic

Step

  1. boil beans for 2 hours
  2. add chopped carrots and bell pepper in the soup
  3. add coconut milk then lower the heat boil once more for 45 minutes
