kuku

Description

kuku is all about grilled chicken meat, rich in protein, essential for growth

Ingredients

  • 0.5kg of raw chicken breast
  • 0.1kg of chicken masala
  • soya sauce
  • 5 lemon juice

Step

  1. mix together chicken breast, chicken masala , soya sauce and lemon juice
  2. leave the mixture fo 2 hours for marination process to proceed
  3. grill marinated breast for 1 hour at very low heat
