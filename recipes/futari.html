futari

Description

futari is a natural african food constist of highly nutrients perfect for people who are fasting

Ingredients

  • 0.5kg of raw cassava
  • 0.1kg raw groundnut
  • 0.1 litre of coconut milk
  • 5 cinnomon nuts

Step

  1. boil raw cassava at mid heat for 5 minutes
  2. add cinnomon and raw groundnuts boil along for 10 minutes more
  3. add coconut milk then lower the heat boil once more for 5 minutes
